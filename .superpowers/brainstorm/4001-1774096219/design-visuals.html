Visual Specs

Ring Track

1px solid line at 10% depot color opacity. Subtle radial glow behind. Ring radius scales: base 100px, +4px per asset (capped at 140px).

Asset Dots

26px circles. Dark navy fill (#001432). 2px border = status color. Glow shadow matching status. Shows last 2 digits of asset number. Evenly spaced around 360°.

Center Hub

44px circle with gradient fill. Shows depot abbreviation + asset count. Pinned state adds a subtle ring indicator (e.g. green ring glow).

Hover Tooltip

Glassmorphic card appears next to hovered dot. Shows full asset number, status text, and "click to fly →" hint. Connected by a thin line.

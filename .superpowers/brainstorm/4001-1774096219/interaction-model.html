How should the popup stay open?

The current 120px hover dome means the popup vanishes the moment your cursor drifts. What trigger model would work best?

A

Click to pin, click elsewhere to dismiss

Hover still previews, but clicking the depot locks the popup open. Click anywhere else (or press Esc) to close. Most desktop-friendly — familiar from tooltips and map info windows.

B

Click-only (no hover preview)

Remove hover popup entirely. Click depot to open, click elsewhere to close. Simpler, more predictable — but you lose the quick-glance preview on hover.

C

Sticky hover with generous hitbox

Keep hover-based, but expand the dome to cover the popup itself so moving into the list doesn't dismiss it. Add a short delay (200ms) before closing. Lowest-effort fix.

D

Slide-out side panel

Click depot → a panel slides in from the right/left with full asset details. Popup becomes just a brief hover label. Best for information-dense views but takes more screen space.

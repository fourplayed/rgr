Design: Interaction Model & States

How the radial ring behaves across its lifecycle

State Machine
1. Idle
SYD
Normal depot pin + label + count badge. No ring visible.
→
2. Hover Preview
SYD
Ring fades in with dots. Quick status glance. Disappears on mouse leave (200ms delay).
→
3. Pinned (clicked)
SYD
Ring locked open. Dots are clickable (fly-to). Hover dot for tooltip. Esc or click-away dismisses.
→
4. Minimized
S
12 ↗
After fly-to-asset. Tiny pill with depot initial + count. Click to re-expand ring.
Transition Rules
Hover depot → Show ring preview
Click depot → Pin ring open
Click asset dot → Fly to asset, minimize ring
Click minimized pill → Re-expand ring (pinned)
Esc / click map → Dismiss entirely (back to idle)
Click other depot → Close current, open new

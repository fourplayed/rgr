Design: Animations & Transitions

How the ring appears, how dots enter, and how states transition

Animation Choreography
0ms
Ring Track Appears

Ring scales from 0 → full size with opacity 0 → 1. Duration: 300ms. Easing: cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) — the same snappy overshoot used by the current depot card.

100ms
Dots Stagger In

Each dot scales from 0 → 1 and fades in, staggered 30ms apart starting from 12 o'clock going clockwise. Creates a "popcorn" ripple effect. Duration per dot: 250ms. Easing: spring(0.6, 0.8, 0).

hover
Dot Hover

Hovered dot scales 1.3x, glow intensifies. Tooltip fades in (150ms) positioned radially outward from center. Other dots dim to 60% opacity to focus attention.

✈
click
Click Dot → Fly To Asset

Clicked dot pulses bright, then the ring collapses: dots scatter inward to center (200ms), ring shrinks to 0. Simultaneously, the minimized pill fades in at the depot location. Map flies to asset at zoom 14.

S
12
restore
Restore from Minimized

Click pill → pill morphs into center hub (expand animation), ring and dots replay their enter animations. Map flies back to depot to re-center the ring.

✕
esc
Dismiss

Esc or click map → all dots fade out simultaneously (150ms), ring shrinks to 0 (200ms). Returns to idle state. No lingering elements.

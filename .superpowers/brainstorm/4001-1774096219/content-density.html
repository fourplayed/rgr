What should each asset row show?

Currently it's just the asset number (e.g. RGR-T-0042). When the popup is pinned open, you have time to read more. What info matters?

1

Status indicator

Colored dot — green (serviced), amber (maintenance), red (out of service). Quick visual scan.

2

Category icon

Small trailer/dolly icon next to the number so you can tell asset types apart at a glance.

3

Last scanned timestamp

Relative time like "2h ago" or "3d ago" — helps spot stale GPS data.

4

Click row → fly to asset

Clicking an asset in the list flies the map to its location and highlights it.

5

Subtype label

Show the asset subtype (e.g. "Flat Top", "Skeletal") as a small tag.

6

Keep it minimal

Just the number is fine — the popup should stay compact. Add info elsewhere.
